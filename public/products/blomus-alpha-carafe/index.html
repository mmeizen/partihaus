Blomus Alpha Decanting Carafe

minimal curved glass

Made by Blomus, the Alpha carafe is defined by a single curve — the glass swells at the base and narrows upward to a steel stopper. There are no straight lines. The form is arrived at purely through function: the wide base maximizes surface area for decanting, the narrow neck controls pour.

It works equally well for wine, water, or juice. Empty, it reads as a sculptural object. The steel stopper is the only hard element — a deliberate contrast that anchors the softness of the glass.

selected because it makes any table look more considered — and because it's the kind of object that prompts questions from guests.
